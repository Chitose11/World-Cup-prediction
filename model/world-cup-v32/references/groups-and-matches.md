## Best Third-Place Logic

| Scenario | Estimated advancement | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| 4 points | >98% | nearly locked in 12-group structure |
| 3 points, positive GD | ~75% | goal difference decisive |
| 3 points, zero GD | ~50% | goals scored/fair play/FIFA rank matter |
| 3 points, negative GD | ~25% | needs other groups to break favorably |
| 2 points | <10% | almost impossible |

Third-place ranking order: points, goal difference, goals scored, fair-play points, FIFA ranking. Steps 1-3 decide almost all cases.
